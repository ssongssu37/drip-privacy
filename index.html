Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 31, 2026

Drip ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information
When you use Drip, we may collect:

  • Your chosen display name (stored on device only)
  • Native language preference
  • English level preference
  • App usage data (lessons viewed, streak count)

We do NOT collect:

  • Email addresses (unless you contact us)
  • Phone numbers
  • Location data
  • Photos or media
  • Contacts

Device Information
We may collect anonymous device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version for analytics and crash reporting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Personalize your learning experience
  • Display translations in your native language
  • Track your learning progress and streaks
  • Improve app performance and fix bugs
  • Provide customer support

3. Data Storage

All personal preferences and learning progress are stored locally on your device. We do not upload your personal data to external servers unless you explicitly choose to create an account.

4. Third-Party Services

Drip may use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat: For subscription management. Their privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Google Play: For app distribution and in-app purchases.
  • Google Text-to-Speech: For audio pronunciation. No personal data is sent — only the English sentence text.

5. Audio Permissions

Drip requests audio permissions solely for text-to-speech playback functionality. We do not record, store, or transmit any audio from your device's microphone.

6. Children's Privacy

Drip is suitable for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

7. Data Retention

Since data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all app data at any time by uninstalling the app. We do not retain any personal data on our servers.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (stored locally on your device)
  • Delete your data (by uninstalling the app or clearing app data)
  • Opt out of notifications (via app settings)
  • Request information about data we process

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.
